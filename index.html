EDU Africa Experience

"Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever."

Project Overview

Created a full Stack LMS for YMCA Athlone, South Africa, which focused on addressing the digital divide in underserved communities. The project involved using APIs, open-source tools, LAMP stack and more to create an intuitive platform for learners.

Global Learning, Skills Acquired & Achievements

One of the most impactful parts of this experience was learning how to adapt my technical knowledge to suit users with varying levels of digital and traditional literacy. Designing for accessibility forced me to think more critically about user experience and simplicity. I also developed leadership skills by guiding a small team through tight deadlines and cross-cultural collaboration. From debugging backend issues to optimizing frontend responsiveness, the hands-on coding experience refined both my technical skillset and my ability to deliver real-world solutions under pressure.

Highlights & Reflections

One of the most profound aspects of this journey was spending time with the local communities and directly engaging with the students our platform was built to support.
